What is Shoshin?

Shoshin (初心) represents the attitude of openness, eagerness, and lack of preconceptions when studying at an advanced level, just as a beginner would.

Openness to Learning

Approach each practice with fresh eyes, regardless of your experience level. Every technique offers new insights and possibilities for growth.

Humility in Practice

Embrace the journey of continuous learning. In Aikido, there's always more to discover about yourself and the art.

Present Moment Awareness

Stay fully engaged in each moment of training. Shoshin keeps us grounded in the here and now, essential for both Aikido and daily life.
