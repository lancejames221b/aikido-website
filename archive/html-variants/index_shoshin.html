What is Shoshin?

ÂàùÂøÉ (Shoshin) - "Beginner's Mind"

Shoshin is a Zen concept that refers to having an attitude of openness, eagerness, and lack of preconceptions when approaching any subject‚Äîjust as a beginner would.

In Aikido, this mindset is essential. Whether you're taking your first step onto the mat or you've been training for decades, approaching each practice with fresh eyes allows for continuous discovery and growth.

At Genshinkan, we embrace this philosophy wholeheartedly. Our community welcomes complete beginners and seasoned practitioners alike, creating an environment where everyone can learn from each other's perspectives and experiences.
